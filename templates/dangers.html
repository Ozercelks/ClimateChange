⚠️ Dangers of Vehicle Emissions

Vehicle emissions are more than just an environmental issue — they pose serious risks to public health, ecosystems, and climate stability. Here's what you need to know.

🚫 Key Dangers

  • 🫁 Respiratory Problems: Particulate matter and NOx can cause asthma and lung diseases.
  • ☁️ Smog Formation: VOCs and NOx create ground-level ozone, harmful to lungs and visibility.
  • 🌍 Climate Change: CO₂ and methane trap heat, leading to rising temperatures.
  • 🧠 Neurological Damage: Long-term exposure to benzene and heavy metals affects brain development.
  • 🌱 Ecosystem Harm: Acid rain and ground ozone damage crops, forests, and aquatic life.
  • 💔 Cardiovascular Diseases: Fine particles increase heart disease and stroke risk.
